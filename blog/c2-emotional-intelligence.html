Emotional Intelligence

18 mar 18

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

Emotional intelligence is essential within a workplace / group, if maximum working-potential is to be achieved. Being self-aware, empathetic, towards other people's emotions -- in general, just being mindful -- is important to not only them, but to yourself and the success of a team.

"The aim of developing emotional intelligence is to help you optimise yourself and function at an even higher level than what you are already capable of."
In that, emotional intelligence can be seen as supplementary to what you already know / can do; it gives you an additional "edge" as a person and professional.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

Pretty difficult -- easy for your mind to wander / hard to re-focus. There is a sense of relaxation / de-stress, but I'm going to attempt at later at night after unwinding, to see if I find it easier.

Can see how this practice of mindfulness -- even for two-minutes -- can be a good habit to get into. Personally, I find doing completely nothing, other than listening to music perfect for me.
