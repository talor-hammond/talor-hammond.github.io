Meditation Process

29 mar 18

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

Always tend to get caught up on making the design look "perfect", or as close to the wireframe as I could get, rather than focussing on the actual process / idea to do with the redesign. In making it as close to the wireframe / concept as possible, I did learn a lot in order to make it work.

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2 of "Search Inside Yourself"?

To be completely honest, I've never done too much reading / research into meditation, or the mentality behind it and it's benefits.

Can see how it would be a good medium for clearing your head if you ever hit a wall when creating / building an idea.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

Meditation is a practice that's been around for thousands of years. The concept of meta-attention was something that stood out, with the "riding a bicycle" analogy a great way of describing how this idea works.

Happiness is the default state of mind -- with meditation: "We are simply returning the mind to its default state."

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

"Process model of mindfulness meditation".

Starting the meditation process with an intention -- a reason for wanting to meditate -- even as simple as to reduce stress, seems like a good way to get in the right mindset. Given that I've never meditated before, this is more speculation / theory, but I'll be trying this technique when I trial meditation in practice.

Any other musings?

Still really behind the idea of music, or any other outlets that you enjoy to get you in the right mindset for exercise -- whether it be the gym, work, school, or relaxation.
